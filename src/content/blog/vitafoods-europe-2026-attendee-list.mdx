## What Are the Key Themes at Vitafoods Europe 2026?

Referencing specific themes in your outreach emails is the difference between "I saw you're going to Vitafoods" (ignored) and "I noticed you're attending the weight management spotlight at Vitafoods" (reply). The 2026 edition introduces some genuinely new focus areas.

### Weight Management and GLP-1 Trends

This is the big one for 2026. According to [Nutraceutical Business Review](https://nutraceuticalbusinessreview.com/vitafoods-europe-2026-expands-with-new-hall-networking), Vitafoods is adding a dedicated spotlight session on weight management, driven by the explosion of GLP-1 medications like Ozempic and Wegovy. The nutraceutical industry is scrambling to develop complementary products: appetite support supplements, muscle preservation formulas, micronutrient replenishment for patients on GLP-1 drugs. If you sell anything in this space, mention this theme in every outreach email.

### Nutricosmetics

The second new spotlight for 2026 covers beauty-from-within products. Think collagen peptides, astaxanthin, biotin formulations, and skin-health botanicals. This market is growing fast in Europe and Asia, and Vitafoods is giving it dedicated stage time for the first time.

### Pet Nutrition Hub

The expanded Pet Nutrition Hub returns with curated display pods. Pet supplements are one of the fastest-growing segments in the nutraceutical space, and Vitafoods is doubling down. If you sell pet-grade ingredients or pet supplement contract manufacturing, this is your corner of the show.

### Sports Nutrition and Healthy Aging

These are perennial Vitafoods themes, but both continue to evolve. Sports nutrition is pushing into personalized nutrition and cognitive performance, while healthy aging (longevity) intersects with nearly every ingredient category at the show.

### Sustainability and Clean Label

Sustainable sourcing, supply chain transparency, and clean-label formulation come up in almost every conversation on the Vitafoods floor. Not a new theme, but one that keeps gaining urgency with European regulatory pressure.

## Pre-Event Outreach Tips for Vitafoods Europe 2026

Sending a cold email that says "Let's meet at Vitafoods" won't cut it. The inbox of a nutraceutical buyer heading to Barcelona is already full. You need specificity.

**Reference the GLP-1 weight management spotlight.** If your ingredient or service connects to weight management, mention this new theme by name. "I saw Vitafoods is running a spotlight on weight management this year. We've been working with brands reformulating for GLP-1 complementary products..." That's a conversation starter.

**Mention Barcelona.** This is only the second year in Barcelona after 26 years in Geneva. People are excited about the new venue. A casual "Looking forward to Barcelona?" in your opener feels human and timely.

**Be specific about what you want.** Don't ask for "a quick chat." Propose a 15-minute meeting at a specific time and place. "Can I grab 15 minutes at your booth on Wednesday morning?" gets more replies than an open-ended ask.

**Follow up within 24 hours post-event.** This one kills me: I've seen teams wait a week or more after Vitafoods to send follow-ups. By then, your prospect has already had five conversations with your competitors. Be first.
