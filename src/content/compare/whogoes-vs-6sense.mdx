6sense and WhoGoes serve B2B sales teams but at very different price points and for different use cases. Here's an honest comparison to help you decide which fits your needs.

**6sense** is an enterprise ABM platform that uses AI to de-anonymize website visitors, track buying signals across channels, and predict which accounts are ready to buy. It's comprehensive but expensive — typically $50K–$100K+/yr.

**WhoGoes** is an event attendee data platform that provides individual contacts who attended specific trade shows, with verified emails and LinkedIn proof. It's focused and affordable — starting at $29.

| Feature | WhoGoes | 6sense |
|---|---|---|
| **Data type** | Individual contacts with emails | Account-level intent + predictions |
| **Price** | From $29 (pay-as-you-go) | $50,000–$100,000+/yr |
| **Contact info** | Name, email, company, LinkedIn | Account names (contacts extra) |
| **LinkedIn proof** | Yes — verifiable attendance proof | No |
| **Signal types** | Event attendance only | Web visits, content, ads, all channels |
| **Predictive scoring** | No | Yes — AI buying stage prediction |
| **Setup time** | Minutes — browse and unlock | Weeks to months for full deployment |
| **Contract** | No contract — credits never expire | Multi-year enterprise contracts |

## What 6sense Does Well

6sense is a powerful enterprise platform. Its strengths:

- **Multi-channel intent** — Tracks buying signals across web visits, ad engagement, content consumption, and third-party data
- **Predictive AI** — Uses machine learning to score accounts by buying stage (awareness → decision)
- **Website de-anonymization** — Identifies companies visiting your website, even without form fills
- **CRM/MAP integration** — Deep integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, and others
- **Orchestration** — Automate workflows based on buying signals

For enterprise ABM teams with the budget, 6sense is a comprehensive platform.

## Where WhoGoes Wins

WhoGoes excels in areas where 6sense falls short:

### 1. Individual-level data
6sense tells you "Acme Corp is showing buying signals." WhoGoes tells you "Sarah Chen, VP of Sales at Acme Corp, attended [HIMSS 2026](/events/himss-2026) — here's her email and LinkedIn proof."

### 2. Immediate actionability
With 6sense, you get account signals and then need to find the right contacts, research their interests, and craft outreach. With WhoGoes, you download a list of verified contacts with emails and start outreach immediately.

### 3. Price accessibility
At $50K+/yr, 6sense is out of reach for most startups and SMBs. WhoGoes starts at $29 with no contracts, making event-based prospecting accessible to any team size.

### 4. Speed to value
6sense requires weeks of setup, CRM integration, and training. WhoGoes delivers value in minutes — search for an event, preview contacts free, unlock the list.

## Who Should Use 6sense?

6sense makes sense for enterprise companies that:

- Have **$50K+/yr** budget for intent data tooling
- Run **multi-channel ABM programs** that need centralized orchestration
- Need **predictive scoring** to prioritize thousands of accounts
- Want to **de-anonymize website traffic** and track the full buyer journey

## Who Should Use WhoGoes?

WhoGoes is built for teams that:

- Need **individual contacts** from specific trade shows and conferences
- Want **verified emails** and **LinkedIn proof** of attendance
- Prefer **pay-as-you-go** over annual enterprise contracts
- Value **speed** — get attendee lists in minutes, not months
